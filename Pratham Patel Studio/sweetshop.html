Our Legacy

Trusted Since 1985

For over three decades, Shree Krishna Sweets and Farshan has been delighting families across Anand with authentic Indian sweets and farsan. Our journey began with a vision to preserve the rich culinary heritage of India.

We believe in using only the freshest ingredients sourced from local markets, ensuring each item maintains its traditional taste and aroma. Our skilled artisans follow age-old recipes passed down through generations, creating confections that bring joy to every celebration.

With unwavering commitment to hygiene, quality, and authenticity, we've earned the trust of countless families who cherish our sweets for weddings, festivals, and everyday moments of happiness.
